BIOGRAPHY

Always having been intrigued by the fascinating photography of my admires, I decided to obtain a camera of my own. Being able to capture breathtaking views and memorable moments has grown on me to become a hobby of mine. I love taking pictures that inspire me and tell a story. A famous photographer Lewis Hine once said. "If I could tell the story in words, I wouldn't need to lug around a camera." Let each photo take your imagination to worlds that never were.
Throughout the website you will find an assortment of styles and I hope that you can find some that will be of inspiration to you. Most pictures on the website are raw with minimal to no editing or enhancement. Equipment used for all shots is a standard DSLR Canon T3. I hope that you will visit the site frequently as I will constantly be updating my portfolio with new photographs.
